CSS Parts

CSS parts allow for fine-grained styling and theming. They target specific elements inside the component. An element may have multiple parts, and a part may be used on multiple elements.

Name Description
disclosure A group within the accordion
title The title area of a group
panel The content of a group
open A modifier when a disclosure or title is open
closed A modifier when a disclosure or title is closed
