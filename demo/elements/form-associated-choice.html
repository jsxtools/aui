Form Associated Choice Element

A custom element that provides authors an easily stylable, form-associated choice.

Example

Leonardo Donatello Michelangelo Raphael

Leonardo Donatello Michelangelo Raphael

example.html

<a-form-associated-choice>
	<a-option>Leonardo</a-option>
	<a-option selected>Donatello</a-option>
	<a-option>Michelangelo</a-option>
	<a-option>Raphael</a-option>
</a-form-associated-choice>
